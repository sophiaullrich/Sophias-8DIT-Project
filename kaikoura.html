About

The Kaikoura district is located on the east coast of New Zealand. The area is known for its scenic beauty, with the Kaikoura Mountains and the Pacific Ocean both providing stunning views. The district is also home to a number of wildlife, including dolphins, whales, and seals. Visitors to the area can enjoy a range of activities such as whale watching, seal swimming, and hiking.

Pricing

The Kaikoura district is a beautiful and popular area of New Zealand. House prices in the district are high, but vary depending on the specific location. In general, prices start at around NZD 1 million and can go up to around NZD 5 million or more. There are a variety of properties available in the district, from small cottages to large mansions. There is something to suit every taste and budget in the Kaikoura district.

Why Kaikoura?

The Kaikoura District is a great place to live for a number of reasons. Firstly, the scenery is absolutely stunning. The Kaikoura mountains provide a stunning backdrop to the town and the coastline is dotted with beautiful beaches. Secondly, the district is home to a number of great attractions including the Kaikoura seals, whale watching and the Kaikoura Peninsula Walkway. Thirdly, the district has a great community feel and there are plenty of things to do in the area. Finally, the district is a great place to raise a family with good schools and a number of family-friendly activities on offer.
