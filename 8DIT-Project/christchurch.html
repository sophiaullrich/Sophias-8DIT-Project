About

Christchurch City is the largest city in the Canterbury Region of New Zealand. The city is located on the east coast of the South Island, and is the gateway to the region. Christchurch is the largest city in the region with a population of over 380,000 people. The city is home to a number of universities, including the University of Canterbury, Christchurch Polytechnic Institute of Technology, and Lincoln University. Christchurch is also a major tourist destination, with a number of attractions, including the Christchurch Botanic Gardens, the Christchurch Art Gallery, and the Christchurch Cathedral.

Pricing
