About

The Auckland region of New Zealand is home to some of the most beautiful and iconic landmarks in the world. From the stunning beaches of the North Shore to the majestic mountains of the Southern Alps, there is something for everyone to enjoy in this unique corner of the world. Some of the most popular tourist attractions in the Auckland region include the Sky Tower, which offers breathtaking views of the cityscape; the Auckland Harbour Bridge, which is one of the most recognisable landmarks in New Zealand; and the Waitemata Harbour, which is home to some of the best sailing and fishing in the country. If you are looking for a more relaxed pace, there are plenty of parks and gardens to explore, as well as a number of vineyards and wineries. For those who enjoy the outdoors, there are plenty of hiking and biking trails to enjoy, as well as a number of golf courses.

Since the late 1990s, Auckland’s housing market has been one of the most expensive in the world. This is largely due to the city’s strong economy and population growth. Auckland’s high housing prices are a major concern for many people. The city needs to find ways to increase the supply of housing and make it more affordable for people to live in.
