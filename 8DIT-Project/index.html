About

This housing graph website will show you graphs from each district displaying the trend of median housing prices in the district. It is very important for potential home owerrs or buyers to identify where they are and aren't able to afford housing in New Zealand.

Districts

New Zealand is divided into 16 regions for local government purposes. Eleven are administered by regional councils (including the Chatham Islands), and five are administered by territorial authorities, which also perform the functions of regional councils in a limited way. Regional councils are responsible for a range of environmental, economic, and social services.

Pricing

The housing crisis in New Zealand has been a very prevalent and talked about matter in New Zealand society. The unreasonably high housing prices and the lack of people who can afford them has caused devastation throughout New Zealand.
