About

Central Otago is a stunningly beautiful district located in the southern part of New Zealand. The region is renowned for its rugged landscapes, picturesque lakes and mountains, and its warm, dry climate. Central Otago is the perfect place to enjoy outdoor activities such as hiking, biking, and fishing, and there are also plenty of opportunities to taste the local wines and enjoy the region's fantastic food.

Pricing
