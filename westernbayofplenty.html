About

The Western Bay of Plenty district is located in the North Island of New Zealand. It is a rural area that is known for its farming and forestry industries. The district has a population of about 33,000 people. The main town in the district is Tauranga. Other towns include Katikati, Te Puke, and Omokoroa. The district is home to many beaches, including Mount Maunganui and Papamoa Beach.
